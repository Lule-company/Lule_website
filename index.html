Created for school. Designed for the planet.

Lule’s mission is to provide children with responsible and long lasting school materials made from high quality and sustainable fabrics with respect to their health and the environment.

Lule creates sustainable products made from recycled fabrics. For school bags we use recycled cotton whereas for bottles we use recycled aluminum or recycled plastic to create them. The idea being Lule is to offer sustainable products that will evolve with the children over the years

Transparency

Our wish is to offer you the greatest transparency on our production methods. Each product is accompanied by a description explaining its materials, the place where it was produced and the composition of the packaging used to send the products. If you do not find the information you are looking for, do not hesitate to go to the "contact" section. We will be happy to answer you.

Respect

We have designed recycled school supplies with the desire to respect the environment and our planet. Each bag and accessory is recycled and recyclable to limit the use of natural resources.

Durability

With Lule parents can buy a product and send it back to us when it will be worned off. If the product is in good conditions it will be used again and promoted through a second hand tab. If the product is damaged it will be recycled and used again as the main fabric of new products. Durability is key to our strategy
